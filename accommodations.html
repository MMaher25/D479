    Stay Your Way in Taniti

    From beachfront hostels to luxury resorts, Taniti offers the perfect place to rest, recharge, and experience island hospitality.

    Whether you're traveling solo, vacationing with family, or planning a romantic getaway, you'll find a wide range of comfortable, safe, and government-inspected accommodations across the island. Choose from cozy bed and breakfasts, charming family-run hotels, or a four-star resort with all the amenities.

    Featured Attractions

    Coral Bay Resort

    Luxury by the Water

    Relax in style at Taniti’s premier four-star resort, located just steps from Yellow Leaf Bay’s white sand beaches. Enjoy oceanfront dining, spa services, a full-service pool, and guided excursions, all in one elegant, island-style setting.

    Sunrise Guesthouse

    Charming & Family-Owned

    This cozy, locally run bed and breakfast offers warm hospitality, homemade breakfasts, and personalized travel tips from lifelong Tanitians. A perfect base for couples or solo travelers looking to experience authentic island life.

    Traveler’s Haven Hostel

    Budget-Friendly & Social

    Ideal for backpackers and students, Traveler’s Haven provides clean, affordable dorms and private rooms in a vibrant, social atmosphere. Located near Merriton Landing with easy access to nightlife, beaches, and public transport.
