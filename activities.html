    Adventure Awaits on Every Shore

    From rainforest thrills to ocean chills, Taniti is packed with unforgettable experiences for every type of traveler.

    Whether you're zip-lining through jungle canopies, snorkeling in crystal-clear waters, hiking to a volcanic crater, or enjoying the nightlife in Merriton Landing, Taniti offers endless ways to explore, unwind, and make memories. Your perfect island adventure starts here.

    Featured Attractions

    Volcano Hike & Crater Views

    Get Up Close to Nature’s Power

    Trek through lush rainforest trails to Taniti’s active volcano. Guided tours offer safe access to scenic viewpoints, fascinating geology, and photo-worthy landscapes you’ll never forget.

    Snorkeling at Coral Cove

    Dive into Underwater Paradise

    Swim alongside colorful fish and vibrant coral reefs just off Taniti’s coast. Great for beginners and seasoned snorkelers alike, with equipment rentals and guided tours available daily.

    Deep-Sea Fishing Charter

    Catch the Adventure of a Lifetime

    Set out from Merriton Landing on a half- or full-day fishing tour. Local captains know the best spots for tuna, mahi-mahi, and marlin. Everything you need is provided, just bring your sense of adventure.
