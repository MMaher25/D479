    Discover the Heart of Taniti

    From ancient culture to modern fun, Taniti’s attractions offer something for everyone, whether you're exploring the past or making new memories.

    Taniti is more than just beautiful beaches. Dive into local history, explore vibrant art galleries, or enjoy family-friendly fun in Taniti City and Merriton Landing. With a blend of cultural sites, exciting entertainment, and scenic landmarks, there's always something new to discover.

    Featured Attractions

    <

    Taniti History Museum

    Step Into the Island’s Story

    Discover the rich cultural heritage of Taniti through artifacts, exhibits, and interactive displays. A must-visit for history lovers and curious minds of all ages.

    Merriton Art Galleries

    Experience Local Creativity

    Stroll through a collection of vibrant, locally owned galleries showcasing Tanitian art, crafts, and photography. Many pieces are available for purchase as one-of-a-kind souvenirs.

    Taniti City Arcade & Bowling

    Fun for the Whole Family

    Beat the heat with some indoor fun: classic arcade games, bowling lanes, and refreshments make this a perfect stop for families or rainy-day adventures.
